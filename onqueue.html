On Queue

When tasked with the intructions to create an app that solves a problem, the options are endless. According to thefactsite.com the average human spends about 5 years of their life waiting in line. We had the idea to slim down this statistic by tackling the time spent waiting in line at events, bars, clubs, and festivals.

Queue On Queue

After extensive research regarding general feelings about standing in line, the results were far from shocking.... Surprise! Nobody likes waiting. We found that in some cases people will even opt out of going to certain places alltogether if they see that there is a long line.

Group Members: Andi Ives, Haley Knapp, Kaylin Price

Clickable Prototype

The Problem

Our line tracking app was designed to alleviate the hassle and stress of waiting in line at an event. We have observed that the market for this type of application is undersaturated, even non existent. How might we allow event users to easily reduce their fears of missing out due to time management involved in taking line wait times into account?

The Solution

Create a tool that allows users to Live Track line wait times. This will allow user to better delegate their time before an event. Also, adding a feature that allows users to skip the line all together to combat the fear of missing out.
