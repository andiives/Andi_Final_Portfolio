Grapevine

There is a universal sense of shame when you are visiting a place and a local outs you as a tourist. But how do you find local approved events that are off the beaten path?

Hear it through the Grapevine



Group Members: Andi Ives and Kaylin Price

Clickable Prototype

The Problem

After extensive research regarding general feelings about travel we have found that many of our users get a sense of stress when planning things to do on their trip. They want to squeeze as many activities as they can into their vacation time window, but lack the tools to discover and organize events and activities within the group that they are traveling with. Grapevine allows users to discover unlikely and "hidden gem" events while creating a shared itinerary so that each member of their group can be completely satisfied with their trip.
